
Jetpack: Strong vertical impulse that positions you above your enemies.
Grenade: Simple grenade that explodes after a few seconds.
Deathly Laser: Strong laser that destroys everything on its path.
Sticky Grenade: Sticky grenade that sticks to any surface or enemy, it explodes after a few seconds.
Instant Health: Instant healing burst (no affect on shield).
EMP: Area stun to all nearby enemies.
Explosive Mine: Explosive trap that activates when an enemy is very close.
Terrestrial Turret: Tactical turret that shoot at any nearby enemy on the ground.
Anti Aerial Turret: Tactical turret that shoot at any nearby enemy on the air.
